SAFETY & SECURITY
Our campus is monitored by CCTV cameras in classrooms, corridors, and common areas for continuous security.
Fire extinguishers are placed throughout the school for quick response in emergencies.
First‑aid medikits are available in classrooms, labs, and administrative spaces for immediate medical help.
Regular safety drills and training are conducted so students and staff are prepared for emergencies.
Controlled entry and exit points ensure only authorized visitors access the campus.
